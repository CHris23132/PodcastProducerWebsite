Privacy Policy for Podcast Like The Pro's

Last Updated: June 2023

Key Points: Podcast Like The Pro's does not collect personal data. All content is stored locally on your device. We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

Introduction

Welcome to Podcast Like The Pro's. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be processed when you use our application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data handling and your rights concerning your information.

Information We Don't Collect

Podcast Like The Pro's is designed with privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Collect personal information from you
  • Track your usage patterns
  • Store your podcast content on our servers
  • Maintain user accounts or profiles
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Local Storage

All your podcast planning, notes, and content are stored locally on your device. This means:

  • Your content remains private and under your control
  • You maintain ownership of all your content
  • Your data persists between sessions without being transmitted to external servers
  • You are responsible for backing up your own data

Third-Party Services

Our application may interact with third-party services to provide certain features:

  • Search Functionality: When you use search features, queries may be processed through Google's search services. These queries are subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • AI Features: Our AI producer features utilize OpenAI's services for content summarization and suggestions. When you use these features, the specific content being processed is temporarily shared with OpenAI and is subject to their privacy policy.

We do not log or store the search queries or content sent to these third-party services. However, these third parties may have their own data collection and retention policies that are outside our control. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

Data Security

While we don't store your data on our servers, we've implemented reasonable security measures within our application to protect the local storage of your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us through our Support page.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
