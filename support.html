Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my podcast planning?
All your work is automatically saved locally on your device. There's no need to manually save your progress as you work.
Can I export my podcast notes?
Yes! You can export your podcast planning and notes in multiple formats including PDF, plain text, and markdown. Look for the export button in the top right corner of your show page.
How does the AI producer feature work?
Our AI producer analyzes your topic and prepared material, then suggests relevant talking points, facts, and questions to enhance your podcast. It helps keep your content fresh and engaging without replacing your unique voice and perspective.
Is my content secure?
Yes. All your podcast content is stored locally on your device. We don't store your content on our servers, which means you maintain complete control and ownership of your material.
Can I use Podcast Like The Pro's on multiple devices?
Currently, your content is stored on the device where you created it. We're working on a sync feature for a future update that will allow seamless use across multiple devices.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing technical issues with the application:

  • Make sure you're using the latest version of the application
  • Try restarting the application
  • Check that your device meets the minimum system requirements
  • Clear your application cache (Settings > Storage > Clear Cache)

If you continue to experience issues, please contact us with details about the problem, including your device model and operating system version.
